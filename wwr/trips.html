Adventure Calls

For Trips we offer different types of difficulty course so both friends and families may be able to join the adventure you want to go on. It is best to go earlier in the year if you are wanting higher rapid courses since that is when the flow is higher. For kids up to 12 years old, we recommend going on any course that is with the difficulty from Ⅰ-Ⅲ. As for those who are 12 and up in age, any course listed is recommended.
