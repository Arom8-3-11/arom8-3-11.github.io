Montana, USA

Montana is located in the northwestern area of the United States. It shares a border with Canada and has about a million residents. During the winters, it may get to about -30 to -50 degrees Fahrenheit. There are many mountains and valleys where the scenery is magnificent in all seasons, With many animals that roam it's lands. Montana, as well as Wyoming, shares the land of Yellowstone National Park, one of the seven natural wonders of North America.
